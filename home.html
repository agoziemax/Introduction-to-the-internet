CELEBRATING MY PARTNER: Onyekachi Okechukwu

Her Excellency

Onyekachi Okechukwu is a thought led beauty entrepreneur, amazon best selling author,
founder/ creative director Onyx Crest Creatives Limited & The Lash Nerd. Lash Business Consultant & Coach.
She is also a content creator, youtuber and graphics designer
She attended one of Nigeria's leading institution were she sudied computer science before venturing into beauty and creative lansdscape.
She also has a facebook community where she mentors aspiring Lash artists, content creators and multi-talented young ladies.

Onyekachi's Background

  • 1990 - Onyekachi was born to the family of Mr & Mrs Ike in Awka, Anambra State.

  • 2012- Onyekachi graduated from Nnamdi Azikiwe University Awka Anambra State, from the dept of Computer Science.

  • 2013 - She went for NYSC in Ogun State where she served in different capacities.

  • 2014 - She joined Beauty Artelier Africa in Lekki Lagos, where she started her journey into the beauty and creativity world..

  • 2016 - She left Beauty Artelier to open her own beauty and Lash Outfit known as The Lash Nerd.

  • 2016- She wrote her first book on Lash Business, and it became an amazon best seller.

  • 2018 - She started her online community that has continued to grow with new member.
